### рҹҹЈ Feature 1 вҖ” Auto Participants

**Problem jo aap jhel rahe ho:**
> *"Main Zoom meeting join karta hoon вҖ” 500 log hone chahiye вҖ” lekin sirf 12 dikhte hain. Akela host baitha rehta hoon."*

**Ye bot kya karta hai:**
```
Aap bolte ho вҶ’ "500 members chahiye"
Bot karta hai вҶ’  Server chalata hai
                 вҶ’ 500 participants meeting mein aate hain
                 вҶ’ Har participant ka alag naam hota hai
                    (Hindi / English / Aapki Custom List)
                 вҶ’ Zoom aur Teams вҖ” dono par kaam karta hai вң…
```

**Fayda jo seedha milta hai:**
- рҹ“ё Screenshot lo вҖ” **full house dikhega**
- рҹ’ј Clients ko dikhao вҖ” **"itne log active hain"**
- рҹҺӨ Host khud confident rehta hai jab room bhara ho
- рҹ‘Ҙ Real audience bhi zyada engage hoti hai bhare room mein

<br/>

### рҹҹ  Feature 2 вҖ” Auto Reactions вқӨпёҸ рҹ”Ҙ рҹ‘Қ рҹҳ®

**Problem jo aap jhel rahe ho:**
> *"Main bolte bolte ruk jaata hoon вҖ” koi reaction nahi, koi emoji nahi вҖ” lagta hai main khud se baat kar raha hoon."*

**Ye bot kya karta hai:**
```

                 вҶ’ рҹ‘Қ вқӨпёҸ рҹ”Ҙ рҹҳ® рҹҺү вҖ” real-time mein
                 вҶ’ Random timing par вҖ” natural lagta hai
                 вҶ’ Poori meeting tak вҖ” band nahi hota
```

**Fayda jo seedha milta hai:**
- рҹҺҜ Speaker ka **morale high** rehta hai вҖ” woh aur achha deliver karta hai
- рҹ‘Җ Nayi audience dekhti hai reactions вҖ” **"sach mein engaging hai"**
- рҹ“№ Meeting recording mein bhi reactions dikhte hain
- рҹ’Ў Investors/clients sochte hain вҖ” **"community active hai"**
